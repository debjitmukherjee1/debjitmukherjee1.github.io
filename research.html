Approach

The methodology behind every note published below.

01 · Screen

Not chosen for breadth

Coverage is screened against a specific thesis — a mispriced re-rating, a sector inflection, a margin or governance debate — across technology, consumer, payments, banking, IT services and macro, then checked against public filings before a model is opened.

02 · Model

Three statements, then a DCF

A full income statement, balance sheet and cash-flow build from the 10-K/annual report — historical ratio analysis, working-capital and capex assumptions — then a DCF, or an excess-return model for banks given book-value and regulatory capital dynamics.

03 · Value

One number isn't enough

The DCF output is sensitivity-tested across WACC and terminal growth, run through scenario cases, and cross-checked against forward earnings, EV/EBITDA and peer comparables before a target is set.

04 · Publish

A rated, published note

The thesis, numbers and risks go into a full research note with an explicit rating — BUY, ACCUMULATE, HOLD, REDUCE — published alongside the underlying Excel model, not just the conclusion.
